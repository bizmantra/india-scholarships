## Outlines: Top 5 to write first

### 1. "[Scholarship Name] — Last Date to Apply 2026" (template)
- H1: [Name] Last Date to Apply 2026
- Quick facts box: amount, eligibility snapshot, portal link
- Deadline (with "last verified" date stamp)
- What happens if you miss the deadline (extension likelihood, next cycle)
- How to apply — condensed 3-step version
- Link to full scholarship detail page + related scholarships
- CTA: eligibility checker

### 2. "Scholarships for Students Under ₹2.5 Lakh Family Income" (template, income hub)
- H1 + intro: why income limit matters, how it's verified
- Table: scholarship name, amount, state, category, deadline (sortable if possible)
- Section: how to calculate your family income correctly (links to income calculator when built)
- CTA: eligibility checker

### 3. "Scholarship vs Education Loan — Which to Apply For First"
- Intro: the real answer is usually "both, in sequence"
- When to prioritize scholarship applications (deadlines earlier, no repayment)
- When a loan makes sense as a bridge/gap-filler
- Can you combine both? (yes — explain DBT/disbursement mechanics)
- Decision checklist
- CTA: EMI calculator + (future) loan pre-qualifier

### 4. "Why Your Scholarship Money Hasn't Been Credited — DBT Issues Explained"
- Common causes: Aadhaar-bank mismatch, seeding not done, verification pending, fund release delay at dept level
- Step-by-step troubleshooting checklist
- When to escalate (helpline, grievance portal)
- CTA: link to relevant state scholarship helpline subpage

### 5. "Central Government Scholarships (NSP) — Complete List 2026"
- Why NSP is confusing (single portal, many schemes bundled)
- List of major NSP-hosted schemes with one-line eligibility each
- How NSP registration works (once, applies to multiple schemes)
- Common NSP application mistakes
- CTA: eligibility checker, filtered to NSP-tagged scholarships

## Suggested sequencing
1. Ship Phase 1 templates first — near-zero marginal cost per page, validated demand.
2. Write outlines 3 and 4 next (loan + DBT) — direct leadgen tie-in, matches your funding-decision positioning.
3. Fill in Phase 2 scholarship-core articles as backlog capacity allows.
4. Defer Phase 3e (study abroad, course ROI) until core clusters are live.
