# ADR-004

Area: Product
Context: Mobile overlays and subscription forms on listing pages cluttered the screen, causing layout shifts and page load speed drops.
Date: June 28, 2026
Decision Name : Deletion of Persisted CTAs & Detail Page Subscription Boxes
Status: Accepted

### Context

Mobile overlays and subscription forms on listing pages cluttered the screen, causing layout shifts and page load speed drops.

### Conversational Rationale

We decided that user experience and speed are our highest priorities. We completely deleted the sticky mobile CTA overlay and the email SubscribeForm boxes from all detailed scholarship pages. This keeps the layout clean, prevents visual shifts on mobile (83% of our traffic), and lets students focus on the content. Subscription forms are now routed to dedicated landing pages or external triggers.
