Leading scholarship programs have announced crucial dates. Students must check eligibility and apply promptly. The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is the main application platform.

### AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girl Students 2026-27

This scheme supports Indian girl students. It covers those in AICTE-approved technical degree or diploma courses. Applications close on October 31, 2026.

An annual family income below ₹8,00,000 is required. Up to two girl children per family can apply. Selected students receive ₹50,000 per year. This amount helps cover fees and academic expenses.

### AICTE Swanath Scholarship Scheme 2026-27

The Swanath Scholarship aids vulnerable students. It is for orphans or those who lost parents to COVID-19. Wards of martyred armed forces personnel also qualify. Students with critically ill or disabled parents are also eligible.

Applicants must be in an AICTE-approved degree or diploma course. The family income ceiling is ₹8,00,000 per annum. A scholarship of ₹50,000 annually is provided. The deadline for applications is October 31, 2026.

### National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS) 2026-27

NMMS assists meritorious students from weaker sections. It supports students from Class 9 to Class 12. Applications for 2026-27 opened on June 1, 2026. The last date to apply is August 31, 2026.

Students must be in Class 8 at government or aided schools. Their family income should not exceed ₹3,50,000. Qualifying students receive ₹12,000 per year.

### Important Reminders for Applicants

All these scholarships are processed via the National Scholarship Portal (NSP). Ensure your bank account is mapped to your Aadhaar number. This is crucial for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) payments. Timely application and document verification are highly recommended.
