A recent news update highlighted how to check your SATS ID. This ID is critical for the SSP Scholarship in Karnataka. The State Scholarship Portal (SSP) is now accepting applications for the 2026-27 academic year.

### What is SATS ID?

The Student Achievement Tracking System (SATS) ID is a unique nine-digit number. It is assigned to every student in Karnataka's education system. This ID helps track academic records and is vital for scholarships.

### How to Find Your SATS ID

Your school can provide your SATS ID. You may also check online using specific Karnataka SATS portals. You will need your name, date of birth, and school name.

### SSP Scholarship 2026-27 Details

The SSP Portal offers various scholarships. These include schemes for pre-matric and post-matric students. Eligible categories are SC, ST, OBC, Minorities, and EWS.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
*   You must be a permanent resident of Karnataka.
*   Income and caste criteria apply and vary by scheme.
*   Students from Class 1 to PhD can apply.

**Application Deadlines:**
*   Pre-Matric scholarships generally close on September 30, 2026.
*   Post-Matric deadlines vary by department.
    *   Backward Classes Welfare and Tribal Welfare: August 31, 2026.
    *   Minorities Welfare Department: September 30, 2026.
    *   Social Welfare Department: January 31, 2027.
Always verify the latest dates on the official SSP portal.

### How to Apply

Applications are submitted online. Visit `ssp.karnataka.gov.in` for pre-matric scholarships. For post-matric scholarships, use `ssp.postmatric.karnataka.gov.in`. You need to create an account, fill in details, and upload documents. E-attestation of documents by your college is also required.

**Key Documents Required:**
*   Your Aadhaar card (linked to bank account).
*   Your SATS ID.
*   Income and caste certificates with valid RD Numbers.
*   Bank passbook details.
*   Previous year's marks card.
*   Bonafide or admission certificate.

### Checking Application Status

For pre-matric scholarships, visit `ssp.karnataka.gov.in`. Enter your SATS ID to track status. For post-matric scholarships, log in to `ssp.postmatric.karnataka.gov.in`. Your dashboard will show your application status.

### Aadhaar and DBT Reminders

Scholarship funds are disbursed via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). Your Aadhaar number must be linked to your bank account. Ensure your bank account is mapped with NPCI. Visit your bank branch to link your Aadhaar if needed. Only one bank account can receive DBT payments at a time. You can check your Aadhaar-bank linking status on the official NPCI website.
