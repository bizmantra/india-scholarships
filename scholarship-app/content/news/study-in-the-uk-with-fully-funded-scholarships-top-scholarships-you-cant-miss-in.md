Exciting news for Indian students eyeing UK education! Several fully funded scholarships are available for the 2026-27 academic year. These prestigious awards cover most study and living costs in the UK. Start preparing your applications now to boost your chances.

### Leading UK Scholarships for 2026-27

**Chevening Scholarship**
This UK government scholarship funds one-year Master's degrees. Applications for the 2027 intake open in August 2026. The deadline is expected by October 6, 2026. It covers tuition, living costs, and travel. Applicants need an undergraduate degree and two years of work experience. You must also commit to returning to India for two years.

**Commonwealth Master's Scholarships**
This scholarship supports postgraduate study for Commonwealth citizens. Funding is available for the 2026/27 academic year. Some applications for 2026-27 may close by March 2026. Others are expected to open around October 2026 for 2027 entry. It typically covers full tuition, a monthly stipend, and airfare. Eligibility requires Indian citizenship and a strong academic record.

**Rhodes Scholarship**
Study at the University of Oxford with full financial support. The Rhodes Scholarship is highly competitive for postgraduate students. For the 2027 intake, US applications close around October 1, 2026. Indian constituency deadlines are usually earlier, often July-August. Applicants generally need to be 19-24 years old with a first-class degree.

**Gates Cambridge Scholarship**
This scholarship fully funds postgraduate degrees at Cambridge University. For 2027-28 entry, applications open in September 2026. Deadlines vary by course, typically December 2026 or January 2027. It is open to non-UK citizens pursuing PhDs, MLitts, or one-year postgraduate courses. The scholarship covers tuition, living costs, and travel expenses.

### Other Noteworthy Opportunities

The **GREAT Scholarships** offer at least £10,000 towards tuition fees. These are available across many UK universities for postgraduate programs. Many individual UK universities also offer specific scholarships for international students. These can be merit-based or program-specific. Always check university websites for details.

### General Eligibility and Application Tips

Eligibility criteria vary for each scholarship. Common requirements include excellent academic records and English proficiency. Many scholarships also look for leadership potential and community involvement. Work experience is vital for some programs like Chevening.

Start your application process at least 12 months before your course begins. Research scholarships thoroughly based on your chosen course and university. Prepare all required documents carefully, including transcripts, essays, and recommendation letters. A well-crafted personal statement is crucial for competitive awards.

Remember to verify all deadlines on the official scholarship websites. Ensure your documents are accurate and complete before submission.
