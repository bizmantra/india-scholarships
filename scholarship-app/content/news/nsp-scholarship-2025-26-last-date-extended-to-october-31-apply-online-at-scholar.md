The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) for the academic year 2026-27 is now accepting applications. Students can apply for various central and state government scholarships. The portal, scholarships.gov.in, is a unified platform for many schemes.

### Key Dates

Applications for most NSP scholarships opened on June 1, 2026. The last date for fresh applications for most schemes is October 31, 2026. Some Pre-Matric scholarships may have an earlier deadline of August 31, 2026.

### Eligibility Criteria

These scholarships are for Indian nationals. Students from Class 1 up to PhD levels can apply. Eligibility varies by scheme, including income and academic requirements. Students should not hold other major government scholarships.

### Application Process

One-Time Registration (OTR) is compulsory for all applicants. This generates a unique 14-digit ID for your academic career. Visit scholarships.gov.in to register and apply.

### Aadhaar and DBT Reminders

An Aadhaar-linked bank account is essential for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). Ensure your Aadhaar is seeded with the NPCI Mapper. Your biometric lock on Aadhaar must be unlocked for face authentication during OTR. Check your bank's DBT enabled status.
