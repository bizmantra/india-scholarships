**PWSAT 2026 Phase 5 Registration Open Now!**

Physics Wallah (PW) has launched Phase 5 of PWSAT 2026. This scholarship test offers significant fee reductions. Students can secure up to 90% scholarships for coaching programs.

**Important Dates to Remember**

Registration for PWSAT Phase 5 is open. Apply between June 11 and July 31, 2026. The online exam window is from June 15 to July 31, 2026. Offline exams were held on June 28 and July 5, 2026.

**Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Criteria)**

Indian students from Class 7 to 12 are eligible. JEE and NEET aspirants (including droppers) can also apply. Students from any recognized board are welcome. There is no registration fee for this test. However, currently enrolled PW students may not be eligible.

**How to Register for PWSAT 2026 Phase 5**

Visit the official Physics Wallah scholarship portal. Click on "Register for Free" and enter your mobile number. Complete OTP verification and fill out the application form. Choose your preferred exam mode: online or offline. Select your desired exam date and submit the form.

For offline registration, visit a nearby PW centre. Fill out the physical form and submit required documents. Ensure to register early for your chosen slot.

**Scholarship Benefits and Details**

Top performers can receive up to a 90% scholarship. This applies to PW Vidyapeeth and Pathshala coaching fees. This is a great chance for affordable competitive exam preparation.

**Aadhaar/DBT Reminder:**

This is a private scholarship opportunity from Physics Wallah. Government-linked Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) schemes or Aadhaar requirements do not apply here.
