## What Are Polytechnic & Diploma Grants?

Polytechnic and diploma courses give direct technical skills to students after Class 10 or 12.

Many government schemes offer financial aid to cover admission fees, books, and hostel expenses for diploma students.

Both central portals and state welfare departments provide dedicated grants for polytechnic education.

## Top Central Government & AICTE Schemes for Diploma Students

Central schemes offer direct cash support to polytechnic students across India.

### 1. AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girl Students (Diploma)
- **Who can apply**: Girl students admitted to 1st year or 2nd year (lateral entry) diploma courses in AICTE approved polytechnics.
- **Grant Amount**: ₹50,000 per year for tuition fees and book purchase.
- **Income Limit**: Family income must be under ₹8 Lakh per year.
- **Link**: Read full details on [/scholarships/pragati-scholarship-for-girls-aitcte](/scholarships/pragati-scholarship-for-girls-aitcte).

### 2. AICTE Saksham Scholarship for Differently-Abled Students
- **Who can apply**: Differently-abled students (disability above 40%) pursuing diploma engineering courses.
- **Grant Amount**: ₹50,000 every year till course completion.
- **Income Limit**: Annual family income below ₹8 Lakh.

### 3. Central Sector Post-Matric Scholarship Schemes
- **Who can apply**: SC, ST, OBC, and Minority category students in technical diploma courses.
- **Grant Amount**: Full fee reimbursement plus monthly maintenance allowance.

## Major State Government Polytechnic Scholarship Schemes

State governments run dedicated programs for students enrolled in state polytechnic institutes.

### 1. Odisha Sudakshya Scheme for Girls
- **Who can apply**: Girls studying in ITI and Polytechnic institutes in Odisha.
- **Grant Amount**: Complete fee waiver, hostel subsidy, and uniform allowance.
- **Link**: Check eligibility on [/scholarships/sudakshya-scheme-for-girls-itipolyechnic](/scholarships/sudakshya-scheme-for-girls-itipolyechnic).

### 2. State Post-Matric Fee Reimbursement Schemes
- **Maharashtra (MahaDBT)**: EBC and SC/ST students get tuition fee grants for polytechnic diplomas.
- **Karnataka (SSP)**: Post-matric scholarship for technical diploma and ITI students.
- **Uttar Pradesh (UP Scholarship)**: Pre-matric and post-matric assistance for polytechnic diploma courses.

## Eligibility Criteria for Diploma Grants

To get polytechnic grants in 2026, you must fulfill basic rules:

1. **Academic Qualification**: You passed Class 10 or Class 12 with at least 50% marks.
2. **Approved College**: Your polytechnic college must be recognized by AICTE or State Technical Board.
3. **Income Cap**: Most schemes require family income below ₹2.5 Lakh or ₹8 Lakh per year.
4. **Active Bank Account**: Your bank account must be linked with Aadhaar NPCI seeding.

## 4 Easy Steps to Apply for Polytechnic Scholarships

Follow these basic steps to submit your grant application without errors:

### Step 1: Collect Required Documents
Keep your marksheet, polytechnic admission slip, bonafide certificate, income proof, and Aadhaar card ready.

### Step 2: Register on official Portals
Register on NSP (National Scholarship Portal) for central schemes or your respective state portal.

### Step 3: Fill Course & College Details
Select your Polytechnic institute, branch name (e.g. Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Computer), and current semester.

### Step 4: Verification & Direct Benefit Transfer
Submit your application to your college verification officer. Once approved, grant money reaches your bank account directly via DBT.
