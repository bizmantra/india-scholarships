## Selection Rules for PM YASASVI

The PM YASASVI scheme has updated its selection process for 2026.
The national entrance test (YET) is no longer conducted.
Instead, selection is purely merit-based using previous school board marks.

Students applying for Class 9 or 11 scholarships are ranked by marks.
Only students in empanelled schools can apply.
State departments allocate quotas to each school and district.

Explore full details on the [PM Yashasvi Scholarship](/scholarships/pm-yashasvi-scholarship).

---

## Empanelled School List Requirements

To qualify, your school must be officially empanelled by the Ministry.
The official list is hosted on the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
Verify your school's status with your principal before registering.

If your school is not empanelled, you cannot apply.
In that case, you may look at the [Top Class Education Scheme](/scholarships/pm-yasasvi-top-class-education).
For J&K residents, check the [J&K OBC Scheme](/scholarships/pm-yasasvi-jk-obc).

## Interactive Document Verification Checklist

Before applying online, ensure your documents are ready.
Incorrect documents will cause application rejection.

- **Marksheet:** Clear copy of Class 8 or Class 10 marks.
- **Caste Certificate:** Verified OBC, EBC, or DNT certificate.
- **Income Certificate:** Must prove family income is under ₹2.5 Lakhs.
- **Bonafide Certificate:** Signed by your school head.

💡 Pro Tip: Make sure your name on Aadhaar matches your school certificate exactly.
