## What is MahaDBT Scholarship Portal?

MahaDBT stands for Maharashtra Direct Benefit Transfer. 

It is the official portal of the Government of Maharashtra.

Students in Maharashtra get college fee waiver and monthly maintenance money here.

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Application on the official MahaDBT portal is 100% free. Never pay money to agents.

---

## Key MahaDBT Schemes for Maharashtra Students

MahaDBT hosts schemes across multiple state departments.

1. **SC and ST Schemes**: Check the [TRTI (Tribal Research and Training Institute) Scholarship for ST Students](/scholarships/trti-scholarship-maharashtra-st).
2. **OBC and VJNT Schemes**: Apply for the [Maharashtra Post-Matric OBC Scholarship](/scholarships/maharashtra-postmatric-obc).
3. **Merit & Girl Schemes**: High scoring girls can claim the [Maharashtra Girls Merit Scholarship](/scholarships/maharashtra-girls-merit). School SC girls can check the [Savitribai Phule Pre-Matric Scholarship](/scholarships/savitribai-phule-pre-matric-scholarship-sc-girls).

Browse all state schemes on our [Maharashtra Scholarships Hub](/scholarships-in/maharashtra).

## Who Can Apply? (Basic Eligibility Rules)

You must meet 4 simple conditions to qualify:

1. **Domicile**: You must be a permanent resident of Maharashtra state.
2. **Category**: Open for SC, ST, OBC, VJNT, SBC, EWS, and General.
3. **Income Limit**: Family income must be under ₹8 Lakh per year for OBC/EBC.
4. **Attendance**: You need at least 75% attendance in college.

## Step-by-Step Online Application & Renewal Guide

Applying or renewing your form takes only 10 minutes.

### Step 1: Create Account or Log In
Visit the portal `mahadbmahait.gov.in`. Click on **New Applicant Registration**. Enter your Aadhaar number and mobile OTP. 

Existing students must click **Applicant Login** for scholarship renewal.

### Step 2: Fill Personal Profile
Enter your caste details and income certificate number. Select your college name and course.

### Step 3: Upload Scanned Papers
Upload clear scans of your income, caste, and marksheets. Ensure all scanned files are under 256KB.

### Step 4: Submit and Print Form
Select your eligible schemes. Review your form carefully and click **Submit**. Print a copy for your college office.

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Delay Money

- **Aadhaar Not Seeded with NPCI**: Money will fail if bank lacks National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) seeding.
- **Wrong Caste Validity**: ST and SC students must submit valid Caste Validity Certificate.
- **Missing College Approval**: Remind your college clerk to approve your form online.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is there any fee to apply on MahaDBT?
No. Applying on MahaDBT is 100% free.

### Q2: Can Students Apply for Renewal After Failing One Subject?
Yes. You can renew if your college allows next year admission.

### Q3: When does scholarship money reach bank accounts?
Money arrives in 2 installments after college and department approval.
