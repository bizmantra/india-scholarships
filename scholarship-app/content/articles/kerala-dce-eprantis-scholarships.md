## What are Kerala DCE and E-Grantz 3.0 Portals?

Directorate of Collegiate Education (DCE) Kerala offers merit scholarships for college students.
E-Grantz 3.0 is an electronic financial assistance portal for SC, ST, OEC, and OBC students.

Both web portals help students in Kerala pay for college and university education.
The government transfers scholarship money directly to bank accounts using Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Apply only on official government websites. Registration is completely free for all students.

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Rules)

Eligibility depends on your course, income, and category.

- **Resident Rule**: You must be a permanent resident of Kerala state.
- **Academic Rule**: You must study in a recognized school, college, or university.
- **Income Rule for E-Grantz**: No income limit for SC, ST, and OEC students. For OBC students, family income must be under ₹1 Lakh per year.
- **Income Rule for DCE Schemes**: Family income must be under ₹1 Lakh per year for BPL schemes.

## Step-by-Step Online Application Guide

Follow these 4 simple steps to complete your registration online.

### Step 1: Register on E-Grantz 3.0 or DCE Portal
Open the official website `egrantz.kerala.gov.in` or `dcescholarship.kerala.gov.in`.
Click on **New Student Registration**. Type your Aadhaar number and SSLC registration details.

### Step 2: Fill Student Profile Details
Log in using your generated application ID and password.
Enter your mobile number, permanent address, and college admission number.

### Step 3: Upload Scanned Certificates
Upload clear PDF files of your income, caste, and marksheets.
Ensure your scanned files are small and easy to read.

### Step 4: Verify and Submit Form
Check all entered details carefully. Click **Submit** to finalize your application form.
Print a copy of the form and submit it to your college office.

## How to Check Application Status Online

Log in to your E-Grantz 3.0 or DCE student portal account.
Click on **Track Application Status** from the main dashboard.
Enter your Aadhaar number or application ID to view your payment stage.

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Block Scholarship Money

- **Bank Account Not Linked**: Your bank account must be seeded with Aadhaar NPCI.
- **Name Mismatch**: Your name on Aadhaar must match your SSLC certificate.
- **Late College Approval**: Your college office must verify your form before the deadline.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: What is the full form of DCE Kerala?
DCE stands for Directorate of Collegiate Education Kerala.

### Q2: Is there any fee to apply on E-Grantz 3.0?
No. Registration and application on E-Grantz 3.0 is 100% free.

### Q3: Can general category students apply on E-Grantz?
No. E-Grantz is mainly for SC, ST, OEC, and OBC students. General category students can apply for DCE merit schemes.
