## What is Karnataka SSP Post-Matric Scholarship?

The **State Scholarship Portal (SSP)** is the official online portal of the Government of Karnataka. 

If you are a student in Karnataka studying in **Class 11, Class 12, Diploma, ITI, B.Tech, BSc, B.Com, or Post-Graduation**, you can get money for college fees and hostel charges through SSP.

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Money is sent directly to your bank account using Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). You do not need to pay any agent or fee to apply.

---

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Criteria)

To get this scholarship, you must meet these 4 simple conditions:

1. **Karnataka Resident**: You must belong to Karnataka state.
2. **Category**: You belong to **SC, ST, OBC, EBC, or Minority** categories.
3. **Education**: You passed Class 10 and are studying in any college or institute in Karnataka.
4. **Income Limit**:
   - **SC / ST Students**: Family annual income must be **below ₹2,50,000**.
   - **OBC / Category-1 Students**: Family annual income must be **below ₹1,00,000**.

## 4 Easy Steps to Submit Your Form Online

Applying on SSP takes only 15 minutes if your documents are ready.

### Step 1: Register on SSP Portal
Visit the official portal `ssp.postmatric.karnataka.gov.in`. Click on **Create New Account**. Enter your Aadhaar number and mobile OTP to create your Account ID.

### Step 2: Enter SATS ID or College Registration Number
Select your college name, university, and course. The portal will automatically fetch your admission details using your SATS ID or college register number.

### Step 3: Verify Caste and Income Certificate Numbers
Enter your **Caste Certificate RD Number** and **Income Certificate RD Number**. The portal automatically checks your certificates with Nadakacheri database. You do not need to upload physical paper scans if RD number matches!

### Step 4: Submit & Download Receipt
Review all details carefully. Click **Submit Application**. Print the acknowledgement page and submit 1 physical copy to your college scholarship clerk.

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Block Payment (And How to Avoid)

- **Error 1: Bank Account Not Seeded with NPCI**: If your bank account is not seeded with Aadhaar, your money will bounce on PFMS. Ask your bank manager to activate **NPCI Mapper**.
- **Error 2: Name Mismatch in Marksheet & Aadhaar**: Make sure your spelling in Aadhaar matches your SSLC marksheet exactly.
- **Error 3: College Not Verifying Application**: After submitting online, remind your college principal or office clerk to approve your application on the SSP E-Attestation portal.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is there an application fee for SSP?
**No.** Registering on the SSP Karnataka portal is 100% free.

### Q2: What to Do If College Name Is Not Listed on SSP?
Ask your college scholarship branch to register their college code on the Karnataka SSP portal database.

### Q3: When Is Scholarship Money Credited to Bank Accounts?
Money is usually disbursed between December and February after institute and department verification.
