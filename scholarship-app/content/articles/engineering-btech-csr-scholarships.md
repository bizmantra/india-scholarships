## Funding B.Tech Education with CSR Grants

Engineering courses in India require high tuition fees.
Government seats fill fast, and private college fees are high.
Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) grants help students finish 4-year B.Tech degrees without financial worry.

Major Indian companies give scholarships to technical students.
These programs offer financial support and career mentoring.
Students can use money for tuition fees, books, and laptops.

## Top CSR Scholarships for B.Tech & Engineering Students

Here are top corporate CSR scholarships available for Indian engineering students.

### 1. Reliance Foundation Undergraduate Scholarship
* **Grant Amount**: Up to ₹2 Lakhs over 4 years.
* **Eligibility**: First-year B.Tech students with Class 12 score of minimum 60%.
* **Income Cap**: Family income must be under ₹15 Lakhs per year (preference under ₹2.5 Lakhs).
* **Key Feature**: Covers degree cost and offers mentorship networks.
* **Direct Link**: Learn more about the [/scholarships/reliance-foundation-undergraduate-scholarship](/scholarships/reliance-foundation-undergraduate-scholarship).

### 2. Foundation for Excellence (FFE) Scholarship
* **Grant Amount**: Full tuition fee support + skill training allowance.
* **Eligibility**: First-year B.Tech students entering via state or national entrance tests (JEE Main, KCET, MHT-CET, WBJEE).
* **Income Cap**: Family income less than ₹3 Lakhs per year.
* **Key Feature**: Provides soft-skill training and English language courses.
* **Direct Link**: Read details on [/scholarships/foundation-for-excellence-ffe-scholarship](/scholarships/foundation-for-excellence-ffe-scholarship).

### 3. Kotak Kanya Scholarship
* **Grant Amount**: Up to ₹1.5 Lakhs per academic year.
* **Eligibility**: Meritorious female students joining premier engineering institutes (IITs, NITs, premier state colleges).
* **Income Cap**: Family income under ₹6 Lakhs per year.
* **Key Feature**: Dedicated corporate scholarship for female tech leaders.

### 4. Tata Capital Pankh Scholarship
* **Grant Amount**: Up to 80% of tuition fees or ₹50,000 per year.
* **Eligibility**: Undergraduates in professional B.Tech courses scoring over 60% in Class 12.
* **Income Cap**: Family income limit of ₹4 Lakhs per year.
* **Key Feature**: Fast online verification and direct bank transfer.

## How to Apply for Engineering CSR Grants

Follow simple steps to get corporate scholarship funding:

1. **Keep Proof Ready**: Collect income certificates, marksheets, and college fee receipts.
2. **Check Deadlines**: CSR applications open right after college admission rounds end.
3. **Submit Clear Scans**: Upload clear PDF files under 200KB on portal forms.
4. **Prepare for Telephonic Round**: Companies call candidates to confirm financial need.
