## What are Tamil Nadu Post-Matric & Pudhumai Penn Schemes?

The Government of Tamil Nadu offers financial help to college students.
The Pudhumai Penn Scheme gives monthly cash to girl students.
The Post-Matric Scholarship covers college tuition fees for SC, ST, and OBC students.

Both schemes send cash directly to your bank account using Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
You can apply online without paying any service fees.

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: You do not need agents. Application on government portals is 100% free.

---

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Rules)

To get these scholarships, you must meet simple rules:

- **Tamil Nadu Resident**: You must live in Tamil Nadu state.
- **Pudhumai Penn Scheme**: You must be a female student. You studied Class 6 to 12 in a Government school. You are now enrolled in a diploma, degree, or ITI course.
- **TN Post-Matric SC/ST**: You belong to SC, ST, or converted Christian SC categories. Family annual income must be below ₹2.5 Lakh.
- **EVR Nagammai Scheme**: PG female students can get fee exemption under [EVR Nagammai Free Education Scheme](/scholarships/evr-nagammai-scholarship).

You can check if you qualify using our [Eligibility Checker Tool](/tools/eligibility-checker).

## Money & Financial Benefits

Each scheme offers different monetary assistance:

- **Pudhumai Penn Scheme**: Get ₹1,000 every month (₹12,000 per year) directly in your account. Read more on the [Pudhumai Penn Scheme](/scholarships/pudhumai-penn-scheme-tamil-nadu) page.
- **TN Post-Matric SC/ST**: Full compulsory fee reimbursement and maintenance allowance for hostellers and day scholars. Check full details on [Tamil Nadu Post-Matric SC/ST Scholarship](/scholarships/tamil-nadu-postmatric-sc-st).
- **OBC / BC / MBC Post-Matric**: Tuition fee assistance for backward class students with income under ₹2.5 Lakh.

Find all active schemes on our [Tamil Nadu Scholarships Hub](/scholarships-in/tamil-nadu).

## Step-by-Step Online Application Guide

Follow these 4 simple steps to submit your form online:

### Step 1: Visit Official Portal
Open the official Pen Kalvi portal `penkalvi.tn.gov.in` for Pudhumai Penn.
Use the ePASS portal for Post-Matric forms.

### Step 2: Register with Aadhaar
Enter your Aadhaar number and phone number.
Type the OTP received on your mobile.

### Step 3: Upload Required Documents
Upload your e-Sevai caste certificate and income certificate.
Attach your school transfer certificate showing Class 6 to 12 study in govt schools.

### Step 4: Submit to College Principal
Submit the online form.
Print a hard copy and give it to your college officer.

---

## How to Check Payment Status

Open the official website. Click on **Know Your Application Status**.
Enter your student ID or Aadhaar number to view payment progress.

---

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Block Payment

Avoid these 3 common mistakes:

- **Aadhaar Not Seeded**: Your bank account must have NPCI seeding for DBT.
- **Incorrect School TC**: Your Transfer Certificate must prove study in Tamil Nadu Govt schools.
- **Wrong Income Details**: Income certificate must be issued recently by the Tahsildar.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Can I get both Pudhumai Penn and Post-Matric scholarships?
Yes. Eligible female students can receive both benefits together.

### Q2: What is the monthly amount for Pudhumai Penn?
You receive ₹1,000 per month until you finish your college degree.

### Q3: When is the money credited?
Money reaches your bank account every month after college attendance verification.
