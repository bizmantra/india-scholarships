## What is Odisha e-Medhabruti and KALIA Scholarship?

The Government of Odisha offers financial aid to bright students after school. The e-Medhabruti scheme gives cash for college fees. The Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation (KALIA) scheme helps children of farmers pay for higher studies.

Both schemes run on the State Scholarship Portal Odisha (SSP Odisha). Students get money directly into their bank accounts via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: You do not need to pay any agents. Application on the official portal is 100% free.

---

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Criteria)

To get e-Medhabruti, you need at least 55% marks in Class 12. Your total family income must be below ₹8 Lakh per year. You must be a permanent resident of Odisha state.

For KALIA scholarship, your parent must be a registered KALIA farmer. You must study in recognized professional or technical courses in Odisha.

Female students in technical diplomas can also check the [Sudakshya for Girls Child Scholarship](/scholarships/sudakshya-for-girls-child-scholarship-odisha).

---

## Scholarship Amount & Benefits

- **e-Medhabruti Undergraduate (UG)**: Get ₹10,000 every year for 3 years.
- **e-Medhabruti Postgraduate (PG)**: Get ₹15,000 every year for 2 years.
- **e-Medhabruti Technical & Professional**: Get ₹20,000 every year for your course.

KALIA scholarship covers full tuition fees, hostel fees, and admission charges.

## Step-by-Step Online Application Guide

Follow these 4 simple steps to submit your online form.

### Step 1: Register on SSP Odisha
Visit the official website `scholarship.odisha.gov.in`. Click on **Registration** and enter your Aadhaar number. Type the One Time Password (OTP) sent to your mobile.

### Step 2: Fill Personal & Academic Details
Log in using your new user ID and password. Select **e-Medhabruti** or **KALIA Scholarship** from the list. Enter your college roll number and Class 12 marks.

### Step 3: Upload Documents
Upload clear scans of your income and domicile certificates. Ensure your uploaded PDF file size is under 2MB.

### Step 4: Submit & Verify Form
Check all your details before clicking **Submit**. Print two copies of the filled form. Submit one copy to your college office for verification.

## How to Check Your Application Status

Open the SSP Odisha website and click **Know Your Status**. Enter your Aadhaar number or application tracking number. Click **Search** to see your current payment stage.

If you want to test your eligibility first, use our [Eligibility Checker Tool](/tools/eligibility-checker). Girls in ITI courses can also view the [Sudakshya Scheme for Girls](/scholarships/sudakshya-scheme-for-girls-itipolyechnic).

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Stop Payments

- **Bank Account Not Linked to Aadhaar**: Your account must have National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) seeding.
- **Wrong Income Certificate**: Your income paper must be issued by the Tahasildar.
- **Pending College Approval**: Your college principal must approve your form online before deadline.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is there an income limit for e-Medhabruti?
Yes. Family income must be under ₹8 Lakh per year.

### Q2: Can I apply for both schemes together?
No. You can only get benefits from one government scheme at a time.

### Q3: When will scholarship money reach my bank?
Money is sent between January and March after college approval.
