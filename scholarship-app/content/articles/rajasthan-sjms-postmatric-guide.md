## What is Rajasthan SJE SJMS Portal?

The **Social Justice and Empowerment (SJE)** Department manages scholarships in Rajasthan.

The **Social Justice Management System (SJMS)** is its online scholarship portal.

Students after Class 10 can get fee money for higher studies.

You apply online using the Single Sign On (**SSO**) portal.

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Apply early to avoid last-minute portal slowdowns.

---

## Schemes Offered on SJE SJMS Portal

The portal hosts major state scholarship schemes for college students.

- **Post-Matric Scholarship**: For SC, ST, OBC, EBC, and SBC students.
- **Chief Minister Scholarship**: For general category needy students.

You can check the [Rajasthan Uttar Matric Scholarship](/scholarships/rajasthan-uttar-matric-scholarship-sc-st-obc) for details.

Also see the [Chief Minister Higher Education Scholarship](/scholarships/chief-minister-higher-education-scholarship-rajasthan) details.

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Criteria)

You must meet basic rules to apply successfully.

1. **Domicile**: You must be a permanent resident of Rajasthan.
2. **Category**: Belongs to SC, ST, OBC, EBC, or SBC category.
3. **Education**: Studying in Class 11, Class 12, ITI, Diploma, or Degree.
4. **Income Limit**:
   - **SC / ST Students**: Family income must be under ₹2,50,000 per year.
   - **OBC / EBC Students**: Family income must be under ₹1,50,000 per year.

Test your eligibility using our [Eligibility Checker Tool](/tools/eligibility-checker).

## 4 Easy Steps to Submit Your Form

Follow these simple steps to fill your application online.

### Step 1: Create SSO ID and Log In
Go to `sso.rajasthan.gov.in`. Register using your Jan Aadhaar Card or Google account. Log in with your new SSO ID and password.

### Step 2: Open Scholarship (SJE) App
Find the **SJMS Portal** or **Scholarship (SJE)** icon on dashboard. Update your profile using your Jan Aadhaar number.

### Step 3: Fill Form & Verify Certificates
Choose your college name, course, and admission year. Enter your caste and income certificate numbers. The portal auto-fetches details from e-Mitra servers.

### Step 4: Upload Receipt & Submit
Upload your current college fee receipt and marksheet. Review your details carefully. Click **Submit Application** and print your receipt.

## ⚠️ 3 Common Mistakes That Stop Funds

- **Bank Account Not Linked**: Seed your bank account with Aadhaar via National Payments Corporation of India (**NPCI**).
- **Jan Aadhaar Details Mismatch**: Name spelling must match your school marksheet.
- **Pending College Approval**: Remind your college clerk to approve your form.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is there an application fee for SJMS portal?
**No.** Application on the official SJE portal is completely free.

### Q2: What is the official website for Rajasthan SJE?
You must apply through `sso.rajasthan.gov.in` portal.

### Q3: When do students receive scholarship money?
Money reaches your account within 2 to 4 months after college approval.
