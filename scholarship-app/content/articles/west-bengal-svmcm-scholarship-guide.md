## What is Swami Vivekananda Scholarship (SVMCM)?

The Swami Vivekananda Merit-cum-Means Scholarship (SVMCM) is a West Bengal state scheme.
It offers financial aid to bright students from poor families.
Students in school, college, and university can get this money.

The scheme helps cover your college fees and course costs.
Money is sent directly to your bank account through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Apply only on the official portal `svmcm.wb.gov.in`. Registration is 100% free for all students.

---

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Rules)

You must fulfill 4 main rules to get this scholarship money:

1. **West Bengal Resident**: You must live permanently in West Bengal state.
2. **Minimum Marks**: You need at least 60% aggregate marks in your last exam. Kanyashree female applicants need 45% marks for PG courses.
3. **Family Income**: Your annual family income must be under ₹2,50,000 (₹2.5 Lakhs).
4. **Regular Student**: You must study full-time in a recognized West Bengal institution.

Students from SC, ST, or OBC categories can also check the [OASIS Scholarship West Bengal](/scholarships/oasis-post-matric-scholarship-for-sc-students-west-bengal).
Girl students receiving K2 benefits can also view [Kanyashree Prakalpa K2](/scholarships/kanyashree-prakalpa-scheme-west-bengal).

## SVMCM Scholarship Amount List

The scholarship money depends on your current course level:

- **Higher Secondary (Class 11 & 12)**: Get ₹1,000 per month (₹12,000 per year).
- **Undergraduate (UG) Arts & Commerce**: Get ₹1,000 per month (₹12,000 per year).
- **Undergraduate (UG) Science & Professional**: Get ₹1,500 per month (₹18,000 per year).
- **Undergraduate Engineering & Medical**: Get ₹5,000 per month (₹60,000 per year).
- **Postgraduate (PG) Arts & Commerce**: Get ₹2,000 per month (₹24,000 per year).
- **Postgraduate (PG) Science & Engineering**: Get ₹2,500 to ₹5,000 per month.

## Step-by-Step Online Application Guide

Follow these 4 simple steps to submit your SVMCM form online.

### Step 1: Register on Official SVMCM Portal
Open the website `svmcm.wb.gov.in`. Click on **Registration** and pick your student category. Enter your basic details and active mobile number to get your Registration ID.

### Step 2: Fill Application Details
Log in with your Application ID and password. Enter your academic marks, course details, and bank account number accurately.

### Step 3: Upload Scanned Documents
Upload clear copies of your marksheet, income proof, and domicile certificate. Make sure your scanned PDF file size is under 400KB.

### Step 4: Final Submission & Verification
Check all details carefully before clicking **Submit**. Print your completed application form. Hand over one copy with paper proofs to your college office.

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Block Payment

Avoid these mistakes to receive your scholarship money without delay:

- **Bank Account Not NPCI Seeded**: Your bank account must be linked with Aadhaar NPCI mapper.
- **Income Certificate Error**: Your income proof must come from a Joint Block Development Officer (BDO) or higher officer.
- **Marks Below 60% Cutoff**: Double-check your overall percentage before submitting the online form.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is 60% marks compulsory for SVMCM?
Yes. You need 60% marks in Class 10, Class 12, or UG exams.

### Q2: What is the annual income limit for SVMCM?
Your family income must be ₹2,50,000 per year or less.

### Q3: When Is SVMCM Money Credited to Bank Accounts?
Money is transferred after college and district officers verify your application.
