## What is UP Scholarship 2026?

The Uttar Pradesh (UP) government offers scholarships for school and college students. 

These schemes help Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), Other Backward Classes (OBC), Minority, and General students.

Applications are filed on the official UP Scholarship portal (`scholarship.up.gov.in`).

Scholarship money goes directly to your bank account via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Online registration on the official portal is completely free. Never pay money to any middleman.

---

## Schemes Offered Under UP Portal

The state offers three main types of scholarship schemes:

1. **Pre-Matric Scholarship**: For students studying in Class 9 and Class 10.
2. **Post-Matric Intermediate Scholarship**: For Class 11 and Class 12 students.
3. **Post-Matric Other Than Intermediate**: For ITI, Diploma, Graduation, and Post-Graduation degrees.

## Eligibility Criteria & Income Limits

To qualify for UP scholarships, you must meet these basic rules:

- **State Resident**: You must be a permanent resident of Uttar Pradesh.
- **Enrolled Student**: You must study in a recognized UP school, college, or university.
- **Income Limit for SC/ST**: Family annual income must be below ₹2.5 Lakh.
- **Income Limit for OBC, General, and Minority**: Family annual income must be below ₹2 Lakh.

## Step-by-Step Online Registration Process

Follow these 4 simple steps to submit your application form:

### Step 1: New Student Registration
Visit `scholarship.up.gov.in`. Select your category and course level. Enter your mobile number, Aadhaar number, and create a password.

### Step 2: Log In & Fill Form
Log in as a Fresh or Renewal student. Enter your personal details, college admission number, and marks.

### Step 3: Upload Scanned Papers
Upload your photograph, caste paper, and income proof. Make sure file sizes match portal limits.

### Step 4: Final Lock & College Submission
Review all entries carefully. Lock your form online. Print the hard copy and submit it to your college within 3 days.

## How to Check UP Scholarship Status Online

You can track your form status in a few quick clicks:

1. Go to `scholarship.up.gov.in`.
2. Click on the **Status** tab on the home page.
3. Select your application year (e.g. 2025-26 or 2026-27).
4. Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth.
5. Click **Search** to see your current verification stage.

## PFMS Verification & Money Transfer

The Public Financial Management System (PFMS) handles all money transfers.

Your bank account must meet these conditions to receive money:

- **Aadhaar Seeding**: Your bank account must link with Aadhaar.
- **NPCI Active**: Activate National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) mapping at your bank branch.
- **Active Account**: Ensure your account is not dormant or closed.

If PFMS shows an error, visit your bank branch immediately.

---

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Reject Applications

- **Aadhaar Name Mismatch**: Name on Aadhaar must match high school marksheets.
- **Bank Account Not NPCI Mapped**: Money transfer fails if NPCI seeding is missing.
- **Unverified College Marks**: College clerk must approve your marks online before deadline.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: What is the official website for UP Scholarship?
The official portal is `scholarship.up.gov.in`.

### Q2: Can General category students apply for UP Scholarship?
Yes. Economically weaker General category students with income below ₹2 Lakh can apply.

### Q3: How do I know if PFMS approved my payment?
Check status on `scholarship.up.gov.in` or visit the official PFMS portal (`pfms.nic.in`).
