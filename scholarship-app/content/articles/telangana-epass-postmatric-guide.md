## What is TS ePASS Post-Matric Scholarship?

The Telangana Electronic Payment and Application System of Scholarships (TS ePASS) is an official portal. It provides financial support to students in Telangana after Class 10.

Eligible students receive fee reimbursement and monthly allowance for higher studies. Money is sent directly to bank accounts through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: You do not need to pay any agents. Application on the official portal is 100% free.

---

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Criteria)

Students must meet these basic rules to get the scholarship:

- **Resident**: You must be a permanent resident of Telangana.
- **Education**: You must study in Class 11, Diploma, Degree, or PG courses.
- **Attendance**: You must maintain at least 75% attendance in college.
- **Income Limits**:
  - SC and ST students: Family income must be under ₹2 Lakh per year.
  - BC and EBC students: Family income must be under ₹1.5 Lakh per year.

Students from Scheduled Castes can check the [Telangana Post-Matric SC Scholarship](/scholarships/telangana-postmatric-sc). 

Students from Backward Classes can view the [Telangana Post-Matric BC Scholarship](/scholarships/telangana-postmatric-bc). 

ST students can read about the [Telangana Post-Matric ST Scholarship](/scholarships/telangana-postmatric-sc).

## Scholarship Amount & Benefits

TS ePASS offers two main benefits:

1. **Reimbursement of Tuition Fee (RTF)**: Full or partial college fees paid to college.
2. **Maintenance Fee (MTF)**: Monthly allowance paid directly to student bank accounts.

Hostel students get higher monthly allowance than day scholars. Check all state schemes on our [Telangana Scholarships Hub](/scholarships-in/telangana).

## Step-by-Step Online Renewal & Fresh Application

Follow these simple steps to complete your registration on TS ePASS.

### Step 1: Visit Official Portal
Open the website `telanganaepass.cgg.gov.in`. Click on **Postmatric Scholarship Services** on the homepage.

### Step 2: Choose Fresh or Renewal
Select **Fresh Registration** for first year studies. Select **Renewal Registration** if you are moving to the next class year.

### Step 3: Fill SSC and Aadhaar Details
Enter your Secondary School Certificate (SSC) hall ticket number. Enter your passing year, date of birth, and Aadhaar number.

### Step 4: Upload Meeseva Certificates
Enter your Meeseva Caste and Income certificate numbers. Upload your college Bonafide Certificate and scanned bank passbook.

### Step 5: Submit and Print Acknowledgement
Check all details carefully before submitting the form. Print the submitted form and give one copy to your college office.

## How to Check TS ePASS Application Status

Visit the TS ePASS portal and click on **Postmatric Scholarship Services**. Click on **Know Your Status** link.

Enter your SSC hall ticket number, application year, and academic year. Click **Get Status** to view your application progress.

You can also test your eligibility with our [Eligibility Checker Tool](/eligibility-checker).

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Stop Payments

- **Aadhaar NPCI Seeding Incomplete**: Bank account must be linked to Aadhaar NPCI mapper.
- **Income Certificate Expired**: Meeseva income certificate must be issued after April 1.
- **College Inspection Pending**: College must verify student biometric attendance online.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is income certificate mandatory every year for TS ePASS?
Yes. Fresh income certificate from Meeseva is required for renewal applications.

### Q2: What is the minimum attendance required for TS ePASS?
Students must have at least 75% attendance in every semester.

### Q3: How is scholarship money transferred to students?
Money is transferred directly to bank accounts using Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
