## What is MPTAAS and Portal 2.0?

The Madhya Pradesh Tribal Affairs Automation System (MPTAAS) is an online portal. It helps Scheduled Caste (SC) and Scheduled Tribe (ST) students get money. Other Backward Classes (OBC) students can also apply for state financial aid.

MP State Portal 2.0 handles all college scholarship forms in Madhya Pradesh. The government sends funds straight to your bank account using direct transfers.

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Always create your MPTAAS profile before college admission ends.

---

## Who Can Apply? (Eligibility Criteria)

You must be a permanent resident of Madhya Pradesh state. You must belong to SC, ST, or OBC categories. You must study in Class 11, college, or university courses.

Your total family income must meet state limit rules. For SC and ST students, the family income limit is ₹6 Lakh per year. You can apply for the [MP TAAS Post-Matric Scholarship](/scholarships/mp-taas-sc-st-postmatric) easily.

High scoring students can also check the [Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana (MMVY)](/scholarships/mukhyamantri-medhavi-vidyarthi-yojana-mp).

## Scholarship Amount & Benefits

The scheme covers full tuition fees for technical and degree programs. Students also receive monthly hosteller or day scholar maintenance allowances.

## Step-by-Step Online Application Guide

Follow these 4 simple steps to complete your MPTAAS application online.

### Step 1: Profile Registration & e-KYC
Visit the official website at `mptaas.mp.gov.in`. Click on User Registration and enter your Samagra ID number. Complete Electronic Know Your Customer (e-KYC) using your Aadhaar OTP.

### Step 2: Fill Academic & Caste Details
Log in with your new User ID and password. Enter your digital caste certificate number and family income details. Enter your college roll number and current admission details.

### Step 3: Upload Required Documents
Upload clear scanned copies of your marksheets and fee receipts. Keep each PDF file size under 2MB before uploading.

### Step 4: Final Submission & College Verification
Double-check all details before clicking the final Submit button. Take a printed copy of your submitted application form. Hand over the form to your college verification counter.

## How to Track Application & Payment Status

Open the MPTAAS portal and click on Track Application Status. Enter your Application Number and academic year to view progress.

You can verify your course criteria using our [Eligibility Checker Tool](/tools/eligibility-checker).

## ⚠️ 3 Common Errors That Stop Payments

- **Bank Account Not Linked**: Your bank account needs Aadhaar NPCI seeding.
- **Name Mismatch**: Your name on Aadhaar and marksheets must match exactly.
- **Incomplete College Approval**: Your institute office must verify your form online.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: What is MPTAAS Portal 2.0?
It is the single online portal for MP tribal and welfare schemes.

### Q2: Is Samagra ID mandatory for MPTAAS?
Yes, you need a valid Samagra ID for profile registration.

### Q3: How will I receive the scholarship amount?
Money is credited directly to your Aadhaar-linked bank account.
