## What is the EWS Income Certificate Limit?

Economically Weaker Section (EWS) certificates help General category students get reservation and financial aid.

However, students often get confused between two numbers: ₹2.5 Lakh and ₹8 Lakh per year.

Understanding this difference is critical before you submit your scholarship application form.

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Use our free [/tools/eligibility-checker](/tools/eligibility-checker) to verify your exact income cutoff before applying.

---

## Why are there Two Different EWS Limits?

The central government sets the main EWS reservation income ceiling at ₹8 Lakh per year.

This ₹8 Lakh limit applies to job reservations and entrance exams like NEET or JEE.

However, post-matric scholarship schemes and corporate CSR programs often enforce a stricter limit of ₹2.5 Lakh per year.

---

## Key Differences: ₹2.5 Lakh vs ₹8 Lakh Cutoffs

| Feature | ₹2.5 Lakh Limit | ₹8 Lakh Limit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Focus** | Merit-cum-means scholarships and state fee waivers | Central EWS seat quotas and exams |
| **Target Schemes** | State welfare schemes and CSR scholarships | Govt college admissions & jobs |
| **Asset Check** | Strict check on land and house size | Standard central EWS asset criteria |
| **Eligible Examples** | State post-matric schemes, [/scholarships/tata-capital-pankh-scholarship](/scholarships/tata-capital-pankh-scholarship) | Central university admission quotas |

---

## How Private CSR Schemes Use Income Limits

CSR scholarship programs like [/scholarships/sbi-asha-scholarship](/scholarships/sbi-asha-scholarship) prioritize the poorest students.

Even if you hold an official 8 Lakh EWS certificate, private trusts may reject candidates earning above ₹2.5 Lakh or ₹3 Lakh.

Always read the specific guidelines of each scheme before uploading your documents.

---

## Summary Checklist for EWS Applicants

1. **Verify the scheme rule**: Confirm if the limit is ₹2.5L or ₹8L.
2. **Check certificate validity**: Ensure your income certificate is issued for the current financial year.
3. **Keep digital copies ready**: Upload clear PDF copies to prevent form rejection.
