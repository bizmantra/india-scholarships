About this Scholarship

The Post Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students is a flagship scheme by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India. This scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to students belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC) and Scheduled Tribe (ST) categories for pursuing post-matriculation or post-secondary education.

The scheme covers a wide range of courses from Class 11 to post-doctoral research, helping students from economically weaker sections access quality education without financial constraints. The scholarship provides support for tuition fees, maintenance allowance, and other educational expenses.

This is a centrally sponsored scheme implemented through state governments and union territory administrations, ensuring wide reach and accessibility across India.

Application Process
How to Apply

Applications are accepted online through the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).

  1. Visit the National Scholarship Portal at scholarships.gov.in
  2. Register as a new applicant or login with existing credentials
  3. Fill in the application form with accurate details
  4. Upload required documents (see Required Documents section)
  5. Submit the application and note down the application ID
üìÑ
Required Documents
  • Aadhaar card
  • Caste certificate (SC/ST)
  • Income certificate (issued within last 6 months)
  • Previous year mark sheet
  • Current year admission proof
  • Bank account details (passbook copy)
  • Passport size photograph
  • Bonafide certificate from institution
  • Fee receipt from institution

Selection & Renewal
Selection Criteria

Merit-cum-Means based selection. Students are selected based on their academic performance and family income. Priority is given to students with higher marks and lower family income.

Total Awards
Available Scholarships:
No fixed limit (subject to budget allocation)
Renewal Information

The scholarship is renewable every year subject to satisfactory academic performance (minimum 50% marks) and continued eligibility. Students must reapply each academic year through the NSP portal.

Frequently Asked Questions
Can I apply if I'm studying outside Karnataka?
Yes, you can apply as long as you are a domicile of Karnataka. The scholarship is available for students studying anywhere in India.
Is this scholarship renewable every year?
Yes, the scholarship is renewable every year subject to satisfactory academic performance and continued eligibility.
What if my family income exceeds ‚Çπ2.5 Lakh?
Unfortunately, you will not be eligible for this scholarship. However, you may explore other merit-based scholarships that don't have income restrictions.
